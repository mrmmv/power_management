About PowerGuard

PowerGuard is an innovative IoT-based smart energy monitoring and protection system designed to provide real-time monitoring of electrical consumption, detect faults, and prevent electrical hazards. The system integrates hardware and software components to create a comprehensive solution for residential and commercial energy management.
